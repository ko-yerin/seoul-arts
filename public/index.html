Describe

Change Tides III (2023) is the third phase of the collaboration that began with Changing Tides I in 2016 and followed early in the pandemic with Changing Tides 2: Requiem for the Earth in February 2020. Our intention is to balance sound and create a path forward, collectively confronting the changing pandemic and undeniable climate crisis. The challenges facing humanity in the field of science and politics today require an unprecedented level of global and cross-cultural cooperation. As artists, we aspire to work in a spirit similar to creating an out-of-place stage and activating it with new intercultural musical expressions using the light-speed web of fiber-optic nerves spread throughout the planet. Working with a team of visual designers and engineers, an ensemble of 10 Korean and American improvisers distributed on both sites will premiere a new musical composition representing shared artistic intimacy in this collaboration to project our best acoustic aspirations.

Our Performing arts

What We do?

(Re)connect

▶️ Space

Inside the bunker. The screen composed of strips of abstract form shows no noticeable movement.


▶️ Keyword

Signals, connections, hopes, beginnings of communication, anxiety


▶️ Scene Composition

- Depending on the musicians' performance (a song that feels like they start talking to each other carefully again), poems written by AI under the theme of keywords on the screen will be placed on the screen in English and Korean (or other multinational languages).

- Certain words or combinations can respond to the sound of a particular instrument.

(Re)cover

▶️ Space

It is recognized as part of the inside of the theater. There is no big movement on the abstract screen.


▶️ Keyword

Existence other than myself, relationship, communication, complexity, energy increase, dynamic start, movement


▶️ Scene Composition

- The music, which was relatively quiet in the beginning, gradually rises up, and the screen starts to move spontaneously. Among the poems generated on the screen, the alphabet (Korean consonants and vowels) move to complete the word recover, and the poetic words written on the theme of recover come out of the screen and begin to float in the air above the audience's head.

- The screen of the belt also continues to move in different forms (45 degrees right, 45 degrees left, up and down, etc.). Ultimately, it covers the audience in existence.

- People (existences) outside of me begin to be visible. It is recovery.

(Re)imagine

▶️ Space

Through the screen that embraces the audience, the audience moves to a completely different dimension (time and space).


▶️ Keyword

Metaverse, As I Imagined, Festival, Oneness, Impossible, Possible, Weightless, Joy


▶️ Scene Composition

- Anything imaginable is possible. Musical expression also reaches its climax. It gradually builds up and people can move freely in the space of zero gravity. Because it is weightless, it is like underwater and also like the universe.
