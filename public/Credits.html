Seoul Institute of the Arts is a prominent and comprehensive art institute in Korea that offers AFA and BFA degree programs. The institute is one of the most competitive colleges in Korea and the graduates are leaders in their field both nationally and internationally. The Institute continues to be a forerunner in globalization of Korean arts and creation of new forms of arts.

UC San Diego is a student-centered, research-focused, service-oriented public institution that provides opportunity for all. Recognized as one of the top fifteen research universities worldwide, a culture of collaboration sparks discoveries that advance society and drive economic impact.

Read more

CultureHub is a global art and technology community founded by Seoul Institute of the Arts and La MaMa ETC. CultureHub has grown into a global network with studios in New York, Los Angeles, Korea, Indonesia, and Italy, providing connected environments for artists to critically examine our evolving relationship to technology.
